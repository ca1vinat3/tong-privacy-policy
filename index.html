Privacy Policy for tong

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

This privacy policy explains how tong, developed by Calvin Nathan Lichungu under the brand Wafreeka Games, collects, uses, and protects user data.

1. Ad Policy

We use advertisements to support the development of this free game. Ads may appear in various parts of the game such as the main menu or after gameplay sessions.

We currently integrate the following ad networks:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads
  • IronSource
  • Facebook Audience Network
  • AppLovin
  • Chartboost
  • Vungle
  • InMobi

These ad networks may collect device identifiers (like Advertising ID), crash logs, and engagement data to serve personalized and non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

2. Account and Authentication

Our game uses Google Sign-In to authenticate users. When signing in, we collect the following:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name

This data is used only to create and manage your PlayFab account and is not shared with third parties outside our infrastructure providers.

3. Leaderboard and Cloud Data

We use PlayFab to store and manage the following game-related information:

  • High scores and rankings
  • Associated email/user ID
  • Gameplay statistics

This allows players to view leaderboards and resume progress across devices. All data is stored securely by PlayFab, a Microsoft-owned service.

4. Third-Party SDKs

We utilize the following third-party SDKs, each with its own data handling practices:

  • Google AdMob & Firebase
  • Unity Ads
  • IronSource Mediation
  • PlayFab (by Microsoft)
  • Google Play Games Services

Each SDK may collect anonymized usage statistics or crash data. Please refer to their individual privacy policies for more detail.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

7. Request Account & Data Deletion

If you would like your account and all related data associated with the TONG mobile game to be deleted, you can send a request to our support team via email.

Send your request to:

  • lichungunathan@gmail.com
  • wafreekainteractive@gmail.com

What data will be deleted?

  • Your Google Sign-In display name and email
  • Your PlayFab account and associated leaderboard scores
  • Any personal identifiers stored via third-party SDKs

How to format your request:

Email us with the subject line: "Delete my TONG game data" and include the Google account used for login.

We will process your request and delete your data within 14 business days.

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
